Rules:

  1. Users will be required to change their passwords the first time they log in.
  2. Passwords must be eight or more characters long.
  3. They can contain upper- and lowercase letters, numbers, and some special characters such as # ! _ or -.
  4. They cannot contain " / \ [ ] : ; | = , + * ? < or > .
  5. Passwords are case sensitive.
  6. A user's password will expire every 180 days from the date it was last changed.
  7. The system maintains a password history of each user's last 24 passwords used, preventing the user from re-using the last 24 passwords.
  8. The minimum password age is one day. After a user changes their password, they must wait 24 hours before they can change it again.
